Frequently Asked Questions

What's Solveo playground?

Solveo's playground is an integrated webapp to seamlessly test different LLM models such as GPT4, Claude, and Gemini to help with homework, essays, and studying.

How does Solveo help with my homework?

Solveo uses advanced AI technology to help you with various types of assignments. Take a photo of your homework, upload a document, or ask questions directly, and our AI will provide detailed explanations and solutions to help you understand the material better.

Can Solveo help with any subject?

Yes! Solveo can help with math, science, history, English, foreign languages, computer science, and many more subjects. Our AI is trained on diverse educational content to assist students across all academic levels.

Is Solveo free to use?

You can start using Solveo for free with limited features. For full access to all our AI tools and unlimited questions, check out our affordable subscription plans in the pricing section.
